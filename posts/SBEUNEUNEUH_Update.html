Running 2026-07-03

SBEUNEUNEUH Update

Since the club began being covered by a website (SBEUNEUNEUH.RUN), we can say that we have entered a new sphere. This did not happen by chance, but through hard work and, above all, driven by the determination of certain members to complete their first marathon. While the idea of keeping it private crossed the minds of some of them, the secret quickly followed the same path as Polichinelle's. Two dates were circled on the calendar: April 12 for Paul, May 3 for Jeffrey and Bérenger.

As if it were a sign of destiny, it was shortly after his race that all the records fell in London. An event that, in our opinion, was quickly erased from the collective memory. For a long time, people believed it was impossible for a human being to cover 42 kilometres in less than two hours. Nike invested enormous resources in trying to achieve that objective. After losing the sprint battle to Puma and Usain Bolt, the American brand spent years attempting to become the reference point in running culture through applications, events, promotional videos, clothing lines, and more.

Thankfully, just one week after those surreal performances, our two runners from Prague also conquered the 42-kilometre mark.

Throughout the race, he was closely followed by Bérenger, who for a long time was the runner with the greatest number of medals within the club. He too performed admirably in front of his family and with encouragement from magnificent homemade signs. He surpassed himself and proudly represented his loved ones over this mythical distance. It was not an easy race, particularly after an injury suffered during preparation, but it never affected his morale. What pride we felt watching our athletes achieve such an accomplishment and prove to everyone that anything is possible.

Once again, congratulations to all the runners, and a special mention to those who go beyond 42 kilometres.
