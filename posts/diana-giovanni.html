Olympics 25 mai 2024

Diana & Giovanni

If one discipline was to define the complete warrior I guess it would be Pentathlon. Nothing better to prepare for a battle, fencing, shooting but also the natural way to move out, running, swimming and horse show jumping. You can imagine how complete of an athlete you need to be to practice Pentathlon, and yes Diana was one of those. 
Coming from Guatemala, Diana never stop working out, if today she's stop competing on Pentathlon, she's still active (No doubt that if she wants she can represent Czech Republic in Olympic level). If one of you want to lose a swimming race, or get out worked at the gym you can call her. 
Only one person so far has been able to beat her, and this at Climbing.
Giovanni is the best person to compete with her, coming from a background of Martial arts, graded in Judo and Kali Sikaran, he's also the best climber we know (He should also considerate partcipating at Olympics for Czech Republic). 
Agile and aware of his strenght, Giovanni is also used to practice lot of different sports. If we had imagine a combat between this two (to see who's the complete warrior), we found out quite quickly that they rather partner up then be against each other. 
Don't tell us that it's too late for them to participate to the Olympics now that they are in July ... 
They have been the best models we could expect for this photoshoot ! 
Would be great now to see them on each other field, Giovanni on a horse and Diana in kimono. 

Now that you know them better we will let you appreciate the work that has been done with these two super athletes, and in case you think you can beat them at any sport, please contact us in order to organise a competition.
