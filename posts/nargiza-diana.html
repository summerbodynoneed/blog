Olympics 17 juillet 2024

Nargiza & Diana

For those who follow our magazine and our instagram, you know that we often mention Kazakhstan in the sport world. Following our article about Shabalina, we had the chance to approach Nargiza. A lady of many talents ! 

If we knew her as an artsy soul, we discover that she is also Olympic material. 
Let alone her photography skills, or drawing abilities, she’s a product of the longtime investment on cycling in Kazakhstan. Now living in Prague, her cycling level is beyond what you can expect. She allowed us to make her our Olympic representant for cycling, and she might also represent us on triathlon following this « open water » photoshoot. 

She kindly accept to be part of our project without hesitation, and she made a perfect duo with our newly Olympic qualified, following her competition on the 14th of July, Diana. 
Despite the fact that she didn’t started long time ago to compete, her approach to animal helped her to develop quickly into her sport. Of course when you partner with a horse you need to understand him, and vice versa. Who understand animals better than Diana ? This simple fact makes her the best horse rider of the country (to not say the world, but soon). Show jumper, Diana accepted to follow us on our photoset, discovering some talent maybe … we might create a Diana Team for pentathlon (cf. Diana and Giovanni article) 

ALGA !!!! 

Thanks again to this two ladies who accepted to be part of our craziness.
